To Be vs Auxiliary Do/Did/Does

Quick Reminder:
1. Use BE (am/is/are/was/were) for descriptions, locations, or with -ing (Continuous).
2. Use DO/DOES/DID for actions in Simple tenses (questions and negatives).

- 2. My sister not usually go to the gym on Mondays.
? 3. the weather good when you were in London last week?
- 4. They not working at 10 PM yesterday; they were asleep.

+ 6. Look! The students preparing a surprise for the teacher.
