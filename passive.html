Active vs Passive: Level B1

Розкрий дужки, поставивши дієслово у правильну форму (Active або Passive). Звертай увагу на час: Present, Past або Future.
1. Every year, millions of smartphones all over the world. (sell)
2. Apple millions of smartphones every year. (sell)
3. This bridge in 1890. (build)
4. Workers this bridge a long time ago. (build)
5. I three emails this morning. (write)
6. These three emails this morning. (write)
7. Coffee in Brazil. (grow)
8. Farmers in Brazil a lot of coffee. (grow)
9. Someone my bike yesterday. (steal)
10. My bike yesterday. (steal)
11. A new hospital next month. (open)
12. The Mayor a new hospital next month. (open)
13. These photos during our last trip. (take)
14. My sister these photos during our last trip. (take)
15. English in many countries. (speak)
