English Practice: Vocabulary (NMT Style)

Read the text below. For questions (1—5) choose the correct answer (A, B, C or D).

The Great Barrier Reef is the world's largest coral reef system. It is (1) ___ in the Coral Sea, off the coast of Queensland, Australia. This natural wonder is so huge that it can be seen from outer space. It (2) ___ of billions of tiny organisms, known as coral polyps.

The reef supports a wide (3) ___ of life, including many vulnerable or endangered species. Because of its environmental importance, the reef was (4) ___ a World Heritage Site in 1981. However, environmentalists are worried because climate change is (5) ___ a serious threat to its future.

1.
  • A) placed
  • B) located
  • C) stayed
  • D) settled
2.
  • A) consists
  • B) involves
  • C) includes
  • D) contains
3.
  • A) difference
  • B) variety
  • C) amount
  • D) collection
4.
  • A) called
  • B) known
  • C) declared
  • D) advertised
5.
  • A) making
  • B) doing
  • C) posing
  • D) giving
Correct Answers:

1 - B (located), 2 - A (consists), 3 - B (variety), 4 - C (declared), 5 - C (posing)
