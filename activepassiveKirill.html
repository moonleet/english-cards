Active or Passive? 🔄

Подсказка: Подумай, подлежащее делает действие или принимает его.
Present Simple: do/does VS is/are + V3
Past Simple: did VS was/were + V3
1. Many people the Eiffel Tower every year. (visit)
2. The Eiffel Tower by millions of people. (visit)
3. Last night, a thief my neighbor's car. (steal)
4. My neighbor's car last night. (steal)
5. Shakespeare "Romeo and Juliet". (write)
6. "Romeo and Juliet" by Shakespeare. (write)
7. Nowadays, millions of emails every minute. (send)
8. I you an email an hour ago. (send)
9. The chef a special dinner tonight. (prepare)
10. The dinner by a famous chef. (prepare)
11. This house in 1950. (build)
12. My grandfather this house long ago. (build)
13. Plastic bottles our oceans. (pollute)
14. Our oceans by plastic waste. (pollute)
15. Tomorrow, the winner by the judges. (announce)
